A List of Posh Names

By Therizinosaurus Cheloniformis
March 26, 2023

Today, in my unrelenting unoriginality and penchant for baseless, faux-postmodern creative outpourings, I present a list of posh names:

  • Ethel
  • Marcus
  • Rupert
  • Digby
  • Julius
  • Edmund
  • Basil
  • Clement
  • Roger
  • Bertram
  • Howard
  • Leo
  • Ezra
  • Graham
  • Araminta
  • Noah
  • Herbert
  • Philomena

What I really want to talk about is what makes a name posh… offensively middle class. Is it the offhand references to classical culture, without really understanding what they mean? Is it the over-the-top way these names are often uttered (always with an air of superiority)?

I do not know. But what I do know, is that these names are hilarious.
