Every time I am ill, I believe that I will die. I just feel terrible, always... Pain and lethargy and boredom and chills and sneezes and the ceaseless coughing and the boredom. It all just sort of bunches up and refuses to leave you, and clings like a sticky sweat. Sometimes, you are hot, and other times, you are cold, and shiver soooo much, that you have to take deep breaths to control it.

My eyes feel all dry and cracked, as if rivulets of blood snake themselves across the sclera, slowly bubbling out of canyons to the top.

I dissected an eye once, it was not very easy, or fun.
