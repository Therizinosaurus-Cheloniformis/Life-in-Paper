A Blog

By Therizinosaurus Cheloniformis
March 4, 2023

Words have always been my passion. 

 

However... consistently writing things has barely ever been easy for me. Some days, I can write thousands of words, while on other days, I struggle to push out a few hundred. Blogging, or faux-blogging always seemed like a good thing for me to do, as the people reading the blog would keep me accountable.

This blog will probably be a collection/assemblage of small ideas, and/or articles that I have written about things.
