I Propose Some Holidays

By Therizinosaurus Cheloniformis
April 4, 2023

The fact that, a few days ago, it was April Fools day got me thinking about holidays and celebrations. Over the last few months, whenever I find an animal which is interesting and strange enough to warrant a day dedicated to it, I decide on a day, to break up the working year - Roman style.

Binturong Day - 8th of May

A day to celebrate Binturongs (Arctictis binturong) - these beautiful, furry members of their monotypic taxon. They are related to palm civets, and live in southeast Asia. The musk that thy secrete smells like buttered Popcorn.

Maned Wolf Day - 6th of July

A day to celebrate Maned Wolves (Chrysocyon brachyurus) - these lanky canids are wonderful. They live in South America, and hunt in tall grasses. They have a very pungent urine, smelling like Beer Hops.

Tripod Fish Day - 13th of September

A day to celebrate tripod fish (Bathypterois grallator) - these fish live in the abyssal zone, and sit on the ocean floor on modified pelvic and caudal fins. They are very cool.

Falanouc Day - 15th of November

A day to celebrate the Falanouc (Eupleres goudotii) - a mammal related to mongooses, that is endemic to Madagascar. There are two species - the western and eastern. The western Falanouc was only recognised in 2010.
