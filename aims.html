Aims

By Therizinosaurus Cheloniformis
March 5, 2023

I have a few aims for this blog:

 

  • It should help me develop a good writing habit, at lease semi-weekly
    • It should help me cultivate ideas
    • It should help me express myself
  • It should allow me to share some knowledge that I have acquired with the world in an effective way.

I know the ⬆above⬆ may look like a trainee teacher's Learning Objective slide on a PowerPoint™, but I think that they will help me keep focused on what I want the website to be.
